About your host

I created Madrid Minds because some things are just better shared.

I've spent over 20 years in the online chess world, building one of the pioneering chess sites, where we strived to make the game accessible, engaging and genuinely fun for people around the world. Along the way I developed a passion for wines, good food, and the kind of evenings where you meet people worth knowing.

The idea was simple: bring curious people together in a beautiful Madrid setting, enjoy great wines and tapas, and introduce chess the way it deserves to be — not as a complicated game, but as one of humanity's most fascinating inventions.

Good food. Good wine. Good people. And a game that might just change the way you spend your evenings.

— Ruy
