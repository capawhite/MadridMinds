Discover chess in a new light

At Madrid Minds, chess isn't taught — it's shared.

Hosted by an insider with over 20 years in the game, this is an evening of stories, discoveries, and a fresh appreciation for one of the world’s most elegant and enduring games.

No experience needed. No pressure. Just curiosity.

About your host

I created Madrid Minds because some things are just better shared.

I’ve spent over 20 years in the chess industry, helping build an online platform that made the game more accessible, engaging, and genuinely fun for people around the world. Along the way I developed a passion for wines, good food, and the kind of evenings where you meet people worth knowing.

The idea was simple: bring curious people together in a beautiful Madrid setting, enjoy great wines and tapas, and introduce chess in a way that feels fresh, welcoming, and genuinely enjoyable.

— Ruy

Wine, tapas and good conversation

At Madrid Minds, wine and food are never an afterthought. You’ll enjoy excellent wines, paired with carefully chosen tapas, in a setting designed for easy conversation and genuine connection.

Not drinking? A thoughtful non-alcoholic pairing is always available.
